sdpctl license prune

clear the license back (from 30) to 1 day

Synopsis

clear the license back (from 30) to 1 day This command only works on appliances higher or equal to 6.1 (API Version 18)

sdpctl license prune [flags]

Examples

  > sdpctl license prune

  -h, --help   help for prune
